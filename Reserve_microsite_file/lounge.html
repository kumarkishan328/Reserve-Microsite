Lounge


Relax before you take-off with special privileges at airport lounges. Enjoy privileged access to Proprietary lounges, complimentary membership to Priority PassTM and access to other Domestic lounges across the country.

  • American Express Proprietary lounges - Enjoy 3 complimentary visits^ in 6 months to a stylish haven that awaits you at the American Express Lounge located in Terminal 3 at Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi and Domestic Terminal 1C at Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai. These oases of peace with luxurious interiors, spacious armchairs are just what you need before taking to the skies. Adding to the experience is the lavish spread of gourmet dishes covering breakfast and all-day dining
  • Priority PassTM Membership - Get access* to over 700 exclusive VIP airport lounges in over 120 countries regardless of the class and airline you fly. Also enjoy complimentary membership to the Priority Pass Programme at a waived off annual membership fee of US$99, exclusively for you
  • Other Domestic Lounges - Unwind at other domestic lounges located in Domestic and International terminals across the country.

Applicable charges apply per person per visit.
